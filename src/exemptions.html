Tax Exemptions and abatements

These benefits can help you pay less property tax by reducing your property’s assessed value or providing tax credits.

Looking for construction and development benefits?

The Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD) manages property tax incentives for certain new construction projects, renovations, and the purchase of foreclosed properties. Explore these benefits on the HPD website.
